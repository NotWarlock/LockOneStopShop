Intellect

Raw increase to all damage dealt.

Haste

Makes all of the things faster. More casts/melees/ticks = More dps.

Mastery

A large increase to the damage of all of your DoTs, as well as Seed of Corruption.

Critical Strike

Critical Strikes deal twice the damage.

Versatility

A relatively weak multiplier to all of your damage, as well as damage taken reduction.

General Priority:

  • Mastery = Haste
  • Critical Strike
  • Versatility
  • Intellect

Haste pulls notably ahead of Mastery for ST, but Mastery pulls ahead of Haste for cleave/AoE. For a general set you want a healthy balance of the two.

Azerite Traits

To view sims about Azerite Traits please visit the Sims > Azerite page here.

The standouts of the class-specific traits are:

  • Inevitable Demise - Agony ticks give you a stacking damage increase to Drain Life.
  • Wracking Brilliance - A large intellect proc with a high uptime.
  • Sudden Onset - Massively increases the damage Agony deals and also makes it start at 4 stacks.
  • Cascading Calamity - Reapplying Unstable Affliction before it expires gives you a haste buff.
