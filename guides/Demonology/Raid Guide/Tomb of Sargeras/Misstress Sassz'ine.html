Mythic - Misstress Sassz'ine

Another fight revolving around maximzing your DPS on the boss by spawning Wild Imps off other targets. As basically every add will be tanked on top of the boss you want to take Hand of Doom to help facilitate the spreading of Doom easily.
For this fight you will be best off using Sephuz's Secret and either Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning or Kazzak's Final Curse. The reason for Sephuz's Secret is because you can easily proc the effect of this item basically on cooldown with your Felguard's Axe Toss ability on the eels that spawn.
Using your Demonic Circle to either dodge Slicing Tornado or Crashing Wave will be the best use of this ability.
