Tips and Tricks

On this fight you should do your best to maintain Immolate on both targets. Make sure you re-cast this on Atrigan before he casts Bone Saw and on Belac before he casts Fel Squall.

Make sure you cast Havoc when there is 10+ seconds until Atrigan casts Bone Saw and Belac casts Fel Squall. This means you'll avoid gaining unnecessary torment.

Place your Demonic Circle off to the side so you can teleport out if Atrigan casts Calcified Quills on you. This will help your raid while reducing your downtime.
